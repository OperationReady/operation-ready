Deploy your people
better prepared.

Operation Ready is a ready-made pre-deployment training resource. No integration required, no accounts to manage — share the link and your team can start preparing immediately.

Why organisations use it

The preparation your induction assumes

Faster onboarding
When new crew arrive already knowing the environment, the terminology, and the basics of their role — your induction covers the specifics instead of the fundamentals. Operations start better.
Safer first deployments
New crew who understand safety protocols, emergency signals, and their role in high-pressure situations before they arrive make fewer costly mistakes. The preparation gap is a safety gap.
No infrastructure required
No LMS, no IT integration, no admin overhead. Add the link to your pre-deployment checklist and it works. Progress is tracked locally on each device — nothing to manage on your end.
Continuously maintained
Modules are reviewed and updated as operational practice evolves. You're not buying a training package that dates immediately — the content is maintained by people actively doing the work.
How it works

Three steps to deployment-ready crew

1
Select your platform
Choose the platform that matches your operational context — humanitarian SAR, conservation vessel operations, or field deployments. Each platform has its own curriculum built for that environment.
2
Share the link
Add the platform URL to your pre-deployment pack. Crew complete modules on any device, at their own pace, before they board. No accounts, no logins, no tracking from your end.
3
Arrive prepared
Crew who've completed the platform arrive knowing the environment, the terminology, and what's expected. Your induction covers your specifics. Operations start better.
