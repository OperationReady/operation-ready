A note on what this platform is

Operation Ready is awareness-level pre-deployment preparation — not certified training, not medical advice, and not a substitute for your organisation's SOPs or onboard induction.

By using this platform you understand that:

  • This does not replace STCW, first aid certification, or professional training
  • Content is provided as-is — always verify it aligns with your organisation's current practice
  • In the field, follow your vessel master, team leader, and organisation's protocols — not this platform
  • Medical content is awareness-level only — defer to trained medical staff for clinical decisions

No accounts, no tracking. Your progress stays on your device. See our full Terms of Use and Privacy Policy for details.
