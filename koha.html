Operation Ready is offered without charge. If it has been useful to you — before a deployment, during training, or in understanding the work — koha is welcome. Entirely at your discretion.

Koha is a Māori concept of giving — a gift offered freely, not as a transaction. There is no right amount, no minimum, and no expectation. This page exists for people who want to contribute to the platform's continued development and have the means to do so.

Contributions support ongoing module development, operational review, and keeping the platform accessible to humanitarian SAR crew worldwide.
